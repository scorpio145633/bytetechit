Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with BYTEtech.

What are typical project timelines?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while custom software applications may require 3-6 months. During the discovery phase, we provide detailed timelines with milestones so you always know what to expect.

How do you handle communication during projects?

We believe in transparent, regular communication. You'll have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact. We use tools like Slack, email, and video calls for daily updates, and provide weekly progress reports. You'll always have visibility into project status.

How do you manage time zone differences?

Our team in Yerevan (GMT+4) has significant overlap with European and Middle Eastern business hours, and we're flexible with scheduling calls for clients in other regions. For urgent matters, our support team is available 24/7.

What does your IT support cover?

Our IT support includes help desk services, system monitoring, software updates and patches, security management, backup management, performance optimization, and user training. We can customize support packages based on your specific infrastructure and needs.

What happens after project completion?

We provide comprehensive handover including documentation, training sessions, and source code transfer. We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our team remains available for future enhancements and updates.
